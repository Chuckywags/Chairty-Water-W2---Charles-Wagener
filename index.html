Our Story

charity: water funds clean and safe water projects for people around the world. Access to clean water means education, income, and health—especially for women and kids. Your support helps build sustainable wells and local capacity so communities can thrive.

Real Impact: Meet Amina

Amina used to walk miles every day to collect water for her family. Thanks to a new community well, she can attend school and her family is healthier and happier. Clean water changes everything—time, health, and opportunity.
